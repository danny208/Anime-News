Top ten best anime of 2023, ranked

The year 2023 has been a wonderful and exciting year for anime fans as new anime series were released, here are some of the best anime ranked

10. Mashle:magic and muscles

Mashle magic and muscle is a japanese manga series written and ilustrated by Hajime Komoto.The story follows a young teenager named mash who lives deep in the forest oblivious to the ways of the world. Thanks to his daily training he becomes a fitness god, when he is discovered he has no choice but to enroll in magic school.

9. Trigun stampede

Trigun stampede is an action sci fi anime series based on trigun manga series created by Yasuhiro Nightow. The story follows gunman vash the stampede as he struggles to maintain his pacifist ways while avoiding the immense bounty on his head.

8. Mishoku no tensei: jobless reincarnation

Mishoku no tensei: jobless reincarnation is a japanese light novel series by Rifujin na Magonote and ilustrated by Shirotaka. The series is about a jobless and hopeless man who dies after having a sad and withdrawn life and reincarnates in a fantasy world while keeping his memories, determined to enjoy his new life without regrets under the name Rudeus Greyrat.

7. Oshi no ko

Oshi no Ko is a Japanese manga series written by Aka Akasaka and illustrated by Mengo Yokoyari. It follows a doctor and his recently deceased patient who were reborn as twins to a famous Japanese pop idol and navigate the highs and lows of the country's entertainment industry as they grow up together through their lives.

6. Ranking of kings

Ranking of kings is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Sōsuke Tōka. It has been serialized online via Echoes' user-submitted Manga Hack website since May 2017 and has been collected in seventeen tankōbon volumes by Enterbrain. The story follows Bojji, a prince in a world where kings are ranked by their deeds and skills, is considered unfit to be the next king due to his disabilities. He runs into Kage, a shadow being with whom he can converse, but who also takes advantage of Bojji's kindness.

5. Dr stone: New world

Dr. Stone (stylized as Dr.STONE) is a Japanese manga series written by Riichiro Inagaki and illustrated by the South Korean artist Boichi.This manga tells the story of how some people from the Pre-Petrification World try to rebuild civilization in the Petrification Age, the Stone World. One of the main characters' first creations undoes the petrification and is called the stone formula.

4. Hell's paradise

Hell's paradise is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Yuji Kaku. Hell's paradise talks a about squad of prisoners and their guards are sent to investigate a mysterious island. They get stranded there and must rely on each other to survive the island's mysterious and monstrous residents.

3. Demon slayer: swordsmith village arc

It is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotōge, and it is about Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered and his younger sister Nezuko is turned into a demon.

2. Jujutsu kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. Jujutsu Kaisen follows the story of Yuji Itadori, an ordinary boy who crosses paths with Megumi Fushiguro, a Jujutsu Sorcerer searching for a powerful Cursed Object known as Ryomen Sukuna's finger. Unintentionally, Yuji's friends unseal the Cursed Object, attracting dangerous Curses to their location.

1. One piece

Eiichiro Oda, one of the most influential figures in the world of manga, rose to fame by creating the most iconic shonen adventure of modern times — One Piece. The best-selling manga series in history, One Piece has seen unprecedented success since first beginning serialization in 1997. The series focuses on Monkey D. Luffy a young man made of rubber after unintentionally eating a Devil Fruit, who sets off on a journey from the East Blue Sea to find the deceased King of the Pirates Gol D. Roger's ultimate treasure known as the "One Piece", and take over his prior title.
